Volunteer Program

🎯 Program Overview

Our volunteer program connects passionate individuals with meaningful accessibility projects worldwide. Join thousands of volunteers making technology more inclusive for everyone.

🔧 Available Activities

  • Braille Transcription & Document Conversion
  • Accessibility Testing for Web & Mobile Apps
  • Community Training & Workshop Facilitation
  • Translation Services for Accessibility Content
  • Content Creation & Educational Material Development
  • Mentorship for Accessibility Students & Professionals
  • Research & Data Collection for Accessibility Studies
  • Event Organization & Community Outreach

⏰ Time Commitment

  • Flexible hours - work at your own pace
  • Minimum 2 hours per week recommended
  • Short-term projects: 1-4 weeks
  • Long-term commitments: 3-12 months
  • One-time events and workshops available

🎁 What You'll Gain

  • Accessibility expertise and certifications
  • Global network of professionals
  • Hands-on experience with assistive technologies
  • Recognition and volunteer certificates
  • Career development opportunities
  • Make a real impact on lives
